SYNOPSIS FOR DESPICABLE ME 4

Gru is back—but this time, the stakes are personal. After attending a class reunion at his villainous alma mater, Lycée Pas Bon, Gru crosses paths with his old nemesis Maxime Le Mal, a cockroach-enhanced supervillain with a grudge dating back to a stolen talent show act.

When Maxime escapes from prison with help from his partner Valentina, he sets his sights on revenge—specifically targeting Gru’s infant son, Gru Jr., with a grotesque cockroach transformation ray. To protect his family, Gru and Lucy relocate under new identities to a quiet town called Mayflower, where they try to blend in while chaos brews beneath the surface.

Meanwhile, five Minions are selected for a secret AVL experiment to become Mega Minions—superpowered versions of themselves. Predictably, things go hilariously wrong, leading to city-wide destruction and the cancellation of the program.

As Gru navigates suburban life, teenage neighbor Poppy Prescott dreams of becoming a villain herself, adding another layer of mischief to the mix. Between family drama, villainous vendettas, and Minion mayhem, Gru must outwit Maxime and save his loved ones before the cockroach gun finds its mark.
